Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ERPX pricing and billing.

Can I change my billing cycle from monthly to yearly?

Yes, you can switch between monthly and yearly billing at any time. When you switch to yearly billing, you'll save 10% on your total cost. The change will take effect at your next billing cycle.

Can I add or remove modules after I subscribe?

Absolutely! You can add or remove modules at any time. Your billing will be adjusted automatically based on your active modules. There are no long-term contracts or commitments.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our PCI DSS compliant payment gateway.

Is there a cancellation fee?

No, there are no cancellation fees. You can cancel your subscription at any time, and you'll have access to your modules until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked.
